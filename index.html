Arisi Paruppu Sadham

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • Curry leaves, turmeric, salt

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and dal for 20 minutes.
  2. In a pressure cooker, temper with mustard, cumin, red chilies, curry leaves.
  3. Add onion, green chilies, and sauté.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, and cook till soft.
  5. Add rice, dal, salt and 3 cups water. Pressure cook for 3 whistles.
  6. Mix well and serve hot with appalam or pickle.

Chef’s Tip:

Use ghee while tempering for extra flavor.

Ambur Mutton Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton
  • 2 cups seeraga samba rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • Whole spices, chili powder, coriander powder, mint, salt

Instructions:

  1. Marinate mutton with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
  2. Sauté onions and tomatoes with whole spices.
  3. Add marinated mutton and cook till tender.
  4. Add soaked rice and water. Cook until rice is done.

Chef’s Tip:

Cook on dum for 10 minutes for perfect flavor.

Chettinad Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups seeraga samba rice
  • Onion, tomato, ginger garlic paste
  • Mint, coriander, curd
  • Chettinad masala (dry roast: cinnamon, cloves, fennel, pepper)

Instructions:

  1. Grind Chettinad masala and keep ready.
  2. Sauté onion, tomato, ginger garlic paste.
  3. Add chicken, curd, masala powder, herbs. Cook well.
  4. Add rice, water and pressure cook for 2 whistles.

Chef’s Tip:

Add a spoon of ghee while serving for aroma.

Dindigul Thalappakatti Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton or chicken
  • 2 cups short-grain rice (Jeera rice)
  • Lemon juice, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste
  • Freshly ground masala (pepper, clove, cinnamon)

Instructions:

  1. Grind masala and marinate meat with lemon and spices.
  2. Sauté green chilies, paste, and meat.
  3. Add rice and cook with exact water ratio.

Chef’s Tip:

Finish with fried onions and serve with raita.
